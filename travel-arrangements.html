Travel Arrangements

In order to book a room, you must do the following things

  1. Call 867-5309 and ask for Jenny
  2. A low voice should answer the phone and say, "Speaking"
  3. Tell her you need six slices of pizza
  4. She will ask if you want any toppings. Ask for anchovies and mushrooms
  5. Within five business days, you will recieve a mysterious package without a return address
  6. Cut the red wire in the package. DO NOT CUT THE BLUE WIRE!!! It will set up us the bomb.
  7. Send the package to the address listed on the bottom of the box.
  8. Within another five business days, you will recieve a ticket to Mazatlan under the name "William Anderson." You will also recieve your room key.
  9. Go to the airport, take the airplane, and an unmarked van will take you from the airport to the resort.
